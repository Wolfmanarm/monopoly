How to Play Monopoly

  • Each player starts with $1500 and begins on the GO space.
  • On your turn, click Roll Dice and move your token the total number of spaces.
  • If you land on an unowned property, you may choose to buy it.
  • If you land on another player's property, you must pay them rent.
  • Landing on Income Tax or Luxury Tax requires you to pay the bank.
  • Landing on GO TO JAIL sends you directly to Jail.
  • Pass GO to collect $200 from the bank.
  • The goal is to be the last player remaining who is not bankrupt.
